# Stranger

A faith-based space for neurodivergent hearts to find comfort, hope, and biblical truth — reminding every visitor that they are wonderfully made by God.

---

## 🌿 What This Project Is

Stranger is a simple, gentle web app that lets users explore:
- Encouraging articles  
- Emotional support messages  
- Bible verses  
- Neurodivergent‑friendly reflections

## 📚 Neurodivergent‑Friendly Books

Here are some helpful books many neurodivergent people enjoy or learn from:

- **How to ADHD (Jessica McCabe)**  
  https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/059357894X  
  A friendly, practical guide based on the popular YouTube channel.

- **Driven to Distraction (Edward M. Hallowell & John Ratey)**  
  https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/0307743152  
  A classic ADHD book written in an encouraging, hopeful tone.

- **ADHD 2.0 (Hallowell & Ratey)**  
  https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/0399178732  
  Modern ADHD strategies explained in a simple, positive way.

- **The Autism-Friendly Guide to Periods (Robyn Steward)**  
  https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/1785924724  
  A gentle, ND‑friendly guide for autistic teens.

- **The Spectrum Girl’s Survival Guide (Siena Castellon)**  
  https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/1787751837  
  Written by an autistic teen for other autistic teens.

- **Uniquely Human (Barry Prizant)**  
  https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/1476776245  
  A compassionate look at autism that focuses on strengths.
